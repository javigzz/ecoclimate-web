19 bioclimatic layers + 4 raw (monthly) variables at 0.5ยบ resolution

We downloaded 4 raw variables (pr, tas, tasmax, tasmin) monthly simulated from the CMPI5/PMIP3, downscaled they at 0.5ยบ resolution and constructed the 19 bioclimatic variables for past, present and future scenarios. Past and future layers were downscaled from three different baselines (piControl, Historical and Modern).
